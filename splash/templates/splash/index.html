Let’s make wikis awesome again!

We love wikis. But we think they could use a makeover and be even better. Simpler, easier to use, so that anyone can participate.

Our vision is EditKit: a compelling modern alternative to legacy wikis.

Better, faster, stronger

Pleasant, frictionless editing experience. No more reading help pages and struggling with markup.

Sharing photos is just as easy as text.

The Web speaks HTML. EditKit does, too.

Built on the awesome Django framework, EditKit is extensible and ideal for painless integration into your existing website. Developers are people, too.

Make the move and bring all your stuff! We provide a tool to easily import data from legacy wiki engines, such as MediaWiki.
